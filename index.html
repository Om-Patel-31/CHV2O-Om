Stratford Public Library

Bridging the Digital Divide in Stratford & Perth

Equal access to digital resources and learning opportunities.

📖 Our Mission

Established: 1903
Expanded: 1926 & 1975
Goal: Empower through digital access & community.

We foster a welcoming, equitable, and safe space for everyone.

📉 Why It Matters

  • 👵 Seniors lack digital literacy & confidence
  • 🔍 Job seekers face online access barriers
  • 🏡 Rural users struggle with poor connectivity

1 in 4 Canadians face digital literacy challenges.
21% of Ontarians lack devices or internet.

💡 What We Offer

  • 👨‍🏫 Tech tutoring & digital literacy programs
  • 🌐 Free Wi-Fi, computers, & device lending
  • 🔧 Creative MakerSpace access
